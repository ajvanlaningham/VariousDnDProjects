## Erina Defender
*(Small) (Humanoid) *

**Proficiency Bonus:**

### Abilities
 --- 
- **Keen Smell**: The erina has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell.

- **Spines**: An enemy who hits the erina with a melee attack while within 5 feet of it takes 5 (2d4) piercing damage.
